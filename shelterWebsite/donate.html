Windmill Animals has been saved hundreds of animals since 1990. We not only rescue domestic animals but help injured wildlife recover and return to their habitats. Over the years we have helped cats, dogs, guinea pigs, ferrets, horses, squirrels, birds, and even the occasional skunk!

Windmill Animal Rescue operates as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. We deeply appreciate any contribution you can make to our efforts—and so do the animals. Every single donation supports the feeding, healthcare, litter, and safety needs of our residents. We process donations with Paypal to keep all personal information secure and to make the process as simple as possible.

One exceptional aspect of Windmill that sets us apart from some of the larger shelters is how we use our donations. Our rescue is run solely by volunteers. This means that every single penny of every donation directly benefits the animals!

Windmill Animal Rescue is always in need of blankets, beds, and other supplies. Please Contact Us if you have any supplies to donate.

Remember, donations are tax deductible!
