Windmill Animal Rescue is a nonprofit organization devoted to the health and wellbeing of animals. The shelter is housed in the Windmill Feed and Pet Supply Company building in Elko New Market, Minnesota.

Windmill relies on the generosity of its donors and volunteers to keep the shelter functioning. We are always seeking new volunteers to care for the pets and maintain the facilities. Because Windmill Animal Rescue has no paid positions, every donation goes directly to helping the animals. Windmill Animal Rescue is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization and we rely on supporters like you! Learn More
