Ways to Help

Amazon Smile

Want to shop and help support Windmill Animal Rescue at the same time?

Amazon Smile is a version of Amazon that allows you to purchase products and .5% of your sales will go to support Windmill. The Smile page offers the same prices, shopping features and prices as Amazon.com

You will need to sign up for an Amazon account if you do not have one already. When you sign up for Amazon Smile you can search for “Windmill Animal Rescue” and start shopping.

If you already have an account go to Accounts & lists, Your Amazon Smile and search for Windmill Animal Rescue.

You may want to bookmark “Amazon Smile” for future use so you are always using Smile rather than Amazon.com and supporting Windmill Animal Rescue. THANK YOU!

Good Networks

Many people use a search engine every day. If you want to help Windmill simply by using a search engine, GoodSearch is for you.

GoodSearch will donate part of their proceeds to a nonprofit of your choice. (Might we recommend Windmill?) GoodSearch uses the Yahoo search engine, so there is little change if you already search with Yahoo.

GoodSearch is the easiest way to help out every day. They also have a search bar and instructions on how to make GoodSearch your default search engine. They also keep track of how much you have donated while you search!

If you enjoy GoodSearch or even if you prefer a different search engine, there are other ways to give back through the Good Network. If you often shop online or eat out, you can give money back to Windmill by using GoodDining and GoodShop.

Sign up for for a new account and search “Windmill” to support a cause. Now you can search, shop or dine and support Windmill Animal Rescue. Thanks for your support!
